About Bihar Engineering University

Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna is a state-run public university established by the Government of Bihar to oversee and coordinate engineering education across the state. The university aims to ensure uniform academic standards, promote research and innovation, and facilitate quality technical education.

BEU serves as an affiliating university for numerous engineering colleges across Bihar, streamlining curriculum development, examination processes, and academic governance. The university is committed to fostering a culture of excellence and skill development to meet the demands of industry and society.
